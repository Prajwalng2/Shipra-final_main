Q1 WHAT ARE CONSTRUCTION CHEMICALS ?

Construction chemicals are chemical formulations used with cement, concrete or other construction materials at the time of construction to hold the construction material together. … The global construction chemical market can be categorised as: Concrete admixtures. Asphalt modifiers. Adhesives and sealants etc. Some construction chemicals are formulated to specifically repair and reburbish old buildings which also increases the life of the building.
